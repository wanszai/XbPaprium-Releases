# XbPaprium-Releases
This is where you can grab the latest builds of XbPaprium for OG Xbox and Xbox 360

What is XbPaprium?
XbPaprium is a port of modifed GX Plus core to run the ROM of Paprium with online crossplay multiplayer without the need for a live/insignia account or an xlink kai setup.

The flow is simple. Load XbPaprium - Pick offline or online and just play. No fancy setups, no faffing around with NATS, no sign ups.

You need to provide your own paprium.bin and paprium music folder. These should be placed into the same folder as the default.xbe or default.xex depending on your platform.

To play online using an xbox 360 a stealth server is required to prevent your kv from being banned. XbPaprium doesnt use xbox live at all so any offline profile will work - even with our online mode. I dont have a banned console to check with, but im pretty sure XbPaprium will let you on either way we do not use any live functionality at all.
